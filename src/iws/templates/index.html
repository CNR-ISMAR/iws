The I-STORMS project seeks to improve the early warning and civil protection procedures in sea storm emergencies. I-STORMS aims through transnational cooperation to enhance innovative policies and develop joint strategies to safeguard the Adriatic-Ionian area from sea storms.

The SEA-STORMS Web System is a combination of a common data system for sharing measurements and forecasts, a multi-model sea forecasting system, a geoportal and interactive geo-visualization tools to make results available to the general public (Open SEA-STORMS)

The SEA-STORMS Web System was developed within the framework of the project I-STORMS, supported by the Interreg ADRION programme, and the project STREAM, supported by the Interreg ITALY-CROATIA programme, funded under the European Regional Development Fund and IPA II fund.
